Recipe 1

Chicken Riggies


"Food"

Ingredients:
For the Chicken:
1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces Salt and pepper to taste
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup chicken broth

For the Sauce:
2 tablespoons butter
1 onion, diced
1 red bell pepper, sliced
1 green bell pepper, sliced
1 cup crushed tomatoes
1 cup heavy cream
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 cup vodka (optional)
1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (adjust to your spice preference)
Salt and pepper to taste

For the Pasta:
1 pound rigatoni or your favorite pasta
Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions:
Start by cooking the pasta according to the package instructions until it's al dente. Drain and set aside.

Season the chicken pieces with salt and pepper. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for about 30 seconds until fragrant.

Add the chicken to the skillet and cook until it's browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.

In the same skillet, add the butter and sauté the diced onion and sliced bell peppers until they become tender, about 5 minutes.

Pour in the vodka (if using) and allow it to simmer for a few minutes until it reduces by half.

Add the crushed tomatoes, heavy cream, crushed red pepper flakes, and grated Parmesan cheese to the skillet. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.

Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and let everything simmer together for a few minutes to heat through. If the sauce becomes too thick, you can add a bit of chicken broth to reach your desired consistency.

Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.

Serve the chicken riggies sauce over the cooked rigatoni pasta. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired.

Enjoy your homemade Chicken Riggies!

This delicious dish is sure to become a family favorite. The creamy, spicy tomato sauce perfectly complements the tender chicken and pasta. Bon appétit!
