Chicken Alfredo


"Food"

Chicken Alfredo is a creamy and indulgent Italian-American dish that combines tender chicken with a luscious Alfredo sauce. Here's a classic Chicken Alfredo recipe for you to enjoy:
**Ingredients:**

For the Chicken:
- 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
- Salt and black pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning (or dried basil and oregano)

For the Alfredo Sauce:
- 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1 cup whole milk
- 1 1/2 cups grated Parmesan cheese
- Salt and black pepper to taste
- Pinch of nutmeg (optional, for added flavor)

For the Pasta:
- 12 ounces fettuccine pasta or your preferred pasta

**Instructions:**
**Cooking the Chicken:**
1. Season the chicken breasts with salt, black pepper, garlic powder, and Italian seasoning on both sides.

2. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the seasoned chicken breasts and cook for about 6-7 minutes on each side, or until they are fully cooked and have a golden-brown crust. The internal temperature of the chicken should reach 165°F (74°C). Remove the chicken from the skillet, let it rest for a few minutes, then slice it into thin strips.

**Preparing the Alfredo Sauce:**
3. In the same skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

4. Pour in the heavy cream and whole milk. Stir and simmer for a few minutes until the mixture is heated but not boiling.

5. Gradually whisk in the grated Parmesan cheese until the sauce is smooth and creamy. Continue to cook for a few more minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken.

6. Season the sauce with salt, black pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg (if using). Adjust the seasonings to your taste.

**Cooking the Pasta:**
7. While preparing the sauce, cook the fettuccine pasta according to the package instructions until it's al dente. Drain the pasta and set it aside.

**Combining Chicken, Sauce, and Pasta:**
8. Add the sliced cooked chicken to the Alfredo sauce and stir to coat the chicken in the creamy sauce. Heat everything together for a few minutes until it's heated through.

9. Add the cooked fettuccine pasta to the skillet with the chicken and sauce. Toss everything together until the pasta is well coated in the creamy Alfredo sauce.

**Serving:**
10. Serve your Chicken Alfredo hot, garnished with additional grated Parmesan cheese and fresh parsley, if desired.

11. Enjoy your homemade Chicken Alfredo with a side of garlic bread or a crisp salad.

This creamy and rich Chicken Alfredo is a delightful comfort food that's perfect for a special dinner or whenever you're craving a comforting Italian-American classic.
