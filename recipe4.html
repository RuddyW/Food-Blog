Mouth Chicken


"Food"

It sounds like you're looking for a flavorful and mouthwatering chicken recipe. Here's a delicious mouth chicken recipe for you to enjoy:
**Ingredients:**
For the Marinade:
- 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
- 2 tablespoons yogurt
- 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
- 1 teaspoon red chili powder
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
- 1 teaspoon garam masala
- Salt to taste
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

For the Sauce:
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 large onion, finely chopped
- 1 green bell pepper, chopped
- 1 red bell pepper, chopped
- 1 yellow bell pepper, chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
- 2-3 green chilies, slit (adjust to your spice preference)
- 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
- 1 teaspoon coriander powder
- 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to your spice preference)
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
- 1 teaspoon garam masala
- 1/2 cup tomato puree
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- Salt to taste
- Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish

**Instructions:**
**Marinating the Chicken:**
1. In a mixing bowl, combine the yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, salt, and 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil.

2. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade, ensuring they are well coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or you can marinate it for several hours or overnight for even more flavor.

**Cooking the Chicken:**
3. Heat 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil in a large skillet or pan over medium-high heat.

4. Add the marinated chicken pieces to the hot oil and cook until they are browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the cooked chicken from the pan and set it aside.

**Preparing the Sauce:**

5. In the same pan, add a bit more oil if needed. Add the cumin seeds and allow them to sizzle for a few seconds.

6. Add the chopped onions, garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Saut√© until the onions become translucent and the mixture is fragrant.

7. Stir in the chopped bell peppers and cook for a few minutes until they begin to soften.

8. Add the coriander powder, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and garam masala. Mix well to combine the spices with the vegetables.

9. Pour in the tomato puree and cook for a few more minutes until the oil starts to separate from the sauce.

10. Reduce the heat to low, and then add the heavy cream and salt. Stir until the sauce becomes creamy and well incorporated.

11. Return the cooked chicken pieces to the pan, coating them with the sauce. Simmer for an additional 5 minutes to heat the chicken through.

12. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves.

**Serve:**
Serve your mouth chicken hot with rice, naan, or crusty bread. It's a rich and flavorful dish that's sure to satisfy your taste buds. Enjoy!
