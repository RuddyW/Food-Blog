Recipe 9

One-Pan Coconut-Lime Chicken


"Food"

One-Pan Coconut-Lime Chicken is a flavorful and easy-to-make dish that combines tender chicken with the tropical tastes of coconut and zesty lime. Here's a delicious recipe for you to try:

**Ingredients:**

- 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs
- Salt and black pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 small onion, finely chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 red bell pepper, sliced
- 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
- 1 can (14 ounces) coconut milk
- Zest and juice of 2 limes
- 2 tablespoons fresh cilantro, chopped (plus more for garnish)
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon ground coriander
- 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to your spice preference)
- Cooked rice or quinoa for serving

**Instructions:**
1. Season the chicken breasts or thighs with salt and black pepper on both sides.

2. In a large skillet or pan, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat.

3. Add the chicken pieces to the skillet and cook for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they are browned and cooked through. Remove the cooked chicken from the pan and set it aside.

4. In the same skillet, add the finely chopped onion and minced garlic. Saut√© for a couple of minutes until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant.

5. Stir in the sliced red and yellow bell peppers and continue to cook for another 3-4 minutes until they begin to soften.

6. Pour in the coconut milk, lime zest, lime juice, fresh cilantro, ground cumin, ground coriander, and red pepper flakes. Stir to combine all the ingredients.

7. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet, nestling it into the sauce.

8. Reduce the heat to low and let everything simmer together for about 5-10 minutes to heat the chicken through and allow the flavors to meld.

9. Taste the sauce and adjust the seasoning with more salt, black pepper, or red pepper flakes if desired.

10. Serve your One-Pan Coconut-Lime Chicken over cooked rice or quinoa, spooning the flavorful coconut-lime sauce over the top.

11. Garnish with additional fresh cilantro.

12. Enjoy your tropical-inspired, one-pan meal that's bursting with coconut and lime flavors!

This One-Pan Coconut-Lime Chicken recipe is not only delicious but also quick and convenient to prepare, making it a perfect weeknight dinner option. The combination of creamy coconut milk, zesty lime, and aromatic spices creates a delightful and refreshing meal.
