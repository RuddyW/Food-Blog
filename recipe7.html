Recipe 7

Butter Chicken


"Food"

Butter Chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, is a beloved Indian dish known for its creamy tomato-based sauce and tender pieces of chicken. Here's a delicious Butter Chicken recipe for you to enjoy:

**Ingredients:**
For the Marinated Chicken:
- 1.5 pounds (700g) boneless, skinless chicken thighs or breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
- 1 cup plain yogurt
- 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste (or minced ginger and garlic)
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon ground coriander
- 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
- 1 teaspoon garam masala
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil


For the Butter Chicken Sauce:
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 2 large onions, finely chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon ground coriander
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- 1 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to your spice preference)
- 1 can (14 ounces) crushed tomatoes
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- 1/4 cup unsalted butter
- Salt to taste
- Fresh cilantro leaves, for garnish

**Instructions:**

**Marinating the Chicken:**
1. In a bowl, combine the yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, ground cumin, ground coriander, ground turmeric, garam masala, salt, and pepper.

2. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade, ensuring they are well coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or ideally overnight, to let the flavors meld.

**Cooking the Chicken:**
3. Heat 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil in a large skillet or pan over medium-high heat.

4. Add the marinated chicken pieces and cook until they are browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the cooked chicken from the pan and set it aside.

**Preparing the Butter Chicken Sauce:**
5. In the same skillet, add 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil.

6. Add the finely chopped onions and sauté until they become soft and translucent.

7. Stir in the minced garlic and grated ginger, cooking for an additional 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

8. Add the ground cumin, ground coriander, paprika, and chili powder. Sauté for another minute or until the spices release their fragrance.

9. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and simmer the sauce for about 10-15 minutes, or until it thickens and the oil starts to separate from the tomato mixture.

10. Add the cooked chicken pieces to the sauce and let them simmer together for a few minutes to combine the flavors.

11. Stir in the heavy cream and unsalted butter. Simmer for an additional 5-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce is creamy and the chicken is coated.

12. Season the Butter Chicken with salt to taste, and adjust the spices if needed.

**Serving:**
13. Serve your homemade Butter Chicken over steamed rice or with warm naan bread.

14. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves.

15. Enjoy the rich and creamy flavors of Butter Chicken, one of India's most beloved dishes!

This Butter Chicken recipe is sure to be a hit at your dinner table. It's creamy, flavorful, and perfect for sharing with family and friends.
